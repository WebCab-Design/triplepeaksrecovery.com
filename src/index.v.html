Located in beautiful Colorado Springs, Colorado, Triple Peaks Recovery integrates a variety of individual, group, and trauma therapies to treat both substance use disorder and many co-occurring mental health issues. Our six-month addiction treatment program allows each individual within our treatment setting to incrementally move forward in their recovery.
